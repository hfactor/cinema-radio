Cinema Radio

There’s something special about working with a movie in the background. It began with the 3 PM movie on All India Radio, old audio cassettes, and later Kiran TV and YouTube. What once was a simple ritual has now become endless scrolling and choosing, losing focus before the work even begins.

This brings that feeling back. You tune in, and something is already playing, like a channel that was never switched off. No browsing, no queues, no deciding. Just curated bands built around genres, moods, themes, and cinematic energy.

A free, open project for people who work, think, and create with movies in the background. The library grows through the people who use it. Add films, shape Bands, and contribute what you’d want playing in the background.
